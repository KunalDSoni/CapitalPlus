Our Story

Founded with a vision to simplify HR and payroll across India, Capital Plus has grown from a startup into a trusted partner for hundreds of organizations. With presence in over 100 cities and a decade of expertise, we've consistently delivered excellence.

Our journey has been marked by innovation, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success. Today, we serve enterprises, mid-market companies, and startups—all united by the same goal: transforming how they manage their most valuable asset—their people.

Our Mission

We exist to take the complexity out of HR and payroll management. By combining cutting-edge technology with human expertise, we empower organizations to focus on what truly matters—growing their business and nurturing their talent.

Every solution we build, every service we deliver, and every interaction we have is guided by a simple principle: making HR simpler, faster, and more compliant for organizations across India. We're not just a service provider; we're your strategic partner in people management.
